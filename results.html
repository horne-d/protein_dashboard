Results

What does the data show?
Company ordered from:

The results show that the higher amount of people chose to order from Myprotein, with the fewest ordering from Protein Works. By changing the year, Myprotein was the biggest seller every year with the exception of 2014 when this was in fact the least popular choice.

Gender:

Reviewing the data, this shows that Myprotein had the most Male customers. However, female customers favoured both Bulkpowders and Protein Works.

Average spending:

On average, a person tends to spend more with bulkpowders with the lowest average spend for Myprotein. Bulkpowders have the highest average spend year on year, with exception to 2014 when in fact they were the lowest on average. From this, females tend to spend more on average at Bulkpowders with males spending less.

Comparing what you spend and your age:

What this shows is that the higher the age, the less you tend to spend. This pattern will be the same across all brands and both genders.
