What you need to know..

This is a dashboard of data which shows a range of datasets from buying supplements. It helps compare average spends, and looks into a correlation between age and what you spend.

You can adjust the data by clicking on the charts, and in the top right corner, you will see a drop down where you can change the year. There would also be tooltips placed around the dashboard, look out for the placed next to the chart title.

You can also see notes on the Results page, and the full set of data used on the Data page.
